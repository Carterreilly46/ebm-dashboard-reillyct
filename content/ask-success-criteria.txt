# Success Criteria Definition
# Define specific, measurable criteria to evaluate solution effectiveness

## SMART Success Criteria

### Criterion 1: Executive Communication Frequency
- **Specific:** Number of executive communications per week during organizational changes
- **Measurable:** Track all executive messages (video, email, town halls, Q&A sessions)
- **Achievable:** Increase from current 0.5 per week to minimum 2-3 per week
- **Relevant:** Directly addresses the lack of regular leadership communication
- **Time-bound:** Achieve target frequency within 2 weeks of change announcement

### Criterion 2: Employee Role Clarity
- **Specific:** Percentage of employees who clearly understand their role and responsibilities
- **Measurable:** Monthly pulse surveys with 5-point clarity scale
- **Achievable:** Maintain 80% role clarity score during transitions (vs. 45% baseline)
- **Relevant:** Core outcome measuring effectiveness of communication
- **Time-bound:** Achieve within 30 days of increased communication implementation

### Criterion 3: Productivity Maintenance
- **Specific:** Sustained productivity levels during organizational changes
- **Measurable:** Track output metrics, project completion rates, quality scores
- **Achievable:** Maintain 90% of baseline productivity (vs. typical 60-70% drop)
- **Relevant:** Demonstrates that clarity leads to maintained performance
- **Time-bound:** Sustained throughout change period (typically 3-6 months)

## Measurement Framework

### Quantitative Metrics
**Communication Frequency Metrics:**
- Executive messages per week: Minimum 2-3 during active change
- Town hall meetings: Bi-weekly during transition periods
- Q&A response time: < 48 hours for employee questions
- Communication channel diversity: Use of 3+ methods (video, email, live)
- Leadership visibility: Executives present at 80% of team meetings

**Role Clarity Metrics:**
- Role clarity survey score: 80% reporting "clear" or "very clear"
- Questions to HR/managers: 50% reduction in role-related inquiries
- Job description alignment: 90% of employees confirm accuracy
- Decision-making clarity: 75% know their authority level
- Reporting structure understanding: 95% can map their org chart

**Productivity Metrics:**
- Output per employee: Maintain 90% of baseline during change
- Project completion rate: Stay within 10% of pre-change timelines
- Quality scores: No decline below 85% of baseline
- Absenteeism: Keep below 5% increase from baseline
- Time to task completion: Within 15% of standard benchmarks

### Qualitative Indicators
**Communication Quality:**
- Message clarity and consistency across channels
- Tone appropriateness for sensitive organizational changes
- Two-way dialogue opportunities (Q&A, feedback channels)
- Transparency about timelines and decision-making process
- Personalization of messages to different employee groups

**Employee Experience:**
- Reduced anxiety and uncertainty about the future
- Increased confidence in leadership during transitions
- Sense of being valued and kept informed
- Trust that leaders understand employee concerns
- Feeling empowered to ask questions and voice concerns

**Cultural Indicators:**
- Maintained or improved employee engagement scores
- Positive informal conversations about leadership communication
- Reduced rumor mill and hallway speculation
- Increased participation in feedback opportunities
- Stronger sense of organizational identity during change

### Leading vs. Lagging Indicators

#### Leading Indicators (Early signals of progress)
- Week 1: Increase in executive message frequency
- Week 2: Employee awareness of communication channels (survey)
- Week 3: Attendance rates at town hall meetings
- Week 4: Number of questions submitted to leadership Q&A
- Month 1: Early role clarity pulse survey results
- Month 2: Manager confidence in cascading messages
- Month 2: Employee sentiment in informal feedback

#### Lagging Indicators (Final outcomes)
- Month 3: Overall role clarity scores reaching 80%
- Month 3-6: Sustained productivity at 90% of baseline
- Month 6: Voluntary turnover rates vs. industry benchmark
- Month 6: Employee engagement survey results
- Month 9: Post-change performance reviews
- Month 12: Retention of high performers through transition
- Year 1: Successful achievement of change objectives

## Baseline Measurements

### Current State Metrics
**Metric 1:** Executive Communication Frequency = 0.5 messages/week (ad hoc)
**Metric 2:** Employee Role Clarity = 45% report clear understanding
**Metric 3:** Productivity During Change = 60-70% of baseline (typical drop)
**Metric 4:** Employee Confidence = 4.2/10 in leadership during transitions
**Metric 5:** Voluntary Turnover During Change = 18% (vs. 12% normal)

### Data Collection Plan
- Weekly tracking of executive communications (automated via intranet)
- Bi-weekly pulse surveys on role clarity (5 questions, 2-minute survey)
- Monthly productivity metrics from existing performance systems
- Quarterly employee engagement surveys
- HR analytics dashboard for turnover and absenteeism
- Town hall attendance and Q&A participation tracking
- Sentiment analysis of employee feedback channels

## Success Thresholds

### Minimum Acceptable Improvement
- Communication frequency: 1-2 executive messages per week
- Role clarity: 65% of employees report clear understanding
- Productivity: Maintain 75% of baseline during change
- Employee confidence: 6.0/10 in leadership during transitions
- Turnover: Keep voluntary departures below 15%

### Target Performance Level
- Communication frequency: 2-3 executive messages per week
- Role clarity: 80% of employees report clear understanding
- Productivity: Maintain 90% of baseline during change
- Employee confidence: 7.5/10 in leadership during transitions
- Turnover: Keep voluntary departures at normal 12%

### Stretch Goal
- Communication frequency: 3-4+ executive messages per week
- Role clarity: 90% of employees report clear understanding
- Productivity: Maintain 95%+ of baseline during change
- Employee confidence: 8.5/10 in leadership during transitions
- Turnover: Improve to 10% (below normal baseline)

## Stakeholder-Specific Success Criteria

### Management Success Measures
- Reduced time spent answering employee questions about roles/changes
- Higher manager confidence in supporting teams through transitions
- Improved team performance metrics during change periods
- Reduced escalations to HR about uncertainty and anxiety
- Faster achievement of change initiative milestones

### Employee Success Measures
- Clear understanding of role, responsibilities, and expectations
- Reduced anxiety and stress during organizational changes
- Confidence in job security and career path
- Feeling valued and informed by leadership
- Maintained work-life balance during transitions

### HR/Leadership Success Measures
- Lower voluntary turnover during change periods
- Reduced employee relations issues and complaints
- Successful achievement of organizational change objectives
- Maintained or improved employee engagement scores
- Positive reputation as an employer during transitions

## Risk Indicators

### Red Flags
- Communication frequency drops below 1 per week
- Role clarity scores declining or stagnant below 60%
- Productivity drops more than 30% from baseline
- Voluntary turnover spikes above 20%
- Increased absenteeism or stress-related leave

### Mitigation Triggers
- Role clarity scores below 70% for 2+ consecutive weeks
- Productivity decline exceeds 25% from baseline
- Employee sentiment scores drop below 5.0/10
- More than 15% increase in HR inquiries about roles/changes
- Town hall attendance drops below 50% of employees

## Timeline for Assessment

### Short-term (Weeks 1-4)
- Executive communication frequency reaches 2+ per week
- Communication channels established and publicized
- First pulse survey shows awareness of increased communication
- Employee questions submitted to Q&A channels
- Manager training completed on message cascading

### Medium-term (Months 2-3)
- Role clarity scores reach 70-75%
- Productivity stabilizes at 80-85% of baseline
- Employee confidence scores improve to 6.5-7.0/10
- Voluntary turnover trending toward normal rates
- Positive feedback themes emerging in surveys

### Long-term (Months 4-12)
- Full target metrics achievement (80% clarity, 90% productivity)
- Sustained behavioral change in communication patterns
- Employee engagement scores return to or exceed pre-change levels
- Successful completion of organizational change objectives
- Best practices documented for future transitions

## Reporting Framework

### Dashboard Metrics
- Real-time executive communication frequency tracking
- Weekly role clarity pulse survey results
- Monthly productivity trend lines vs. baseline
- Employee confidence/sentiment scores
- Voluntary turnover rates
- Town hall attendance and Q&A participation
- Communication channel usage analytics

### Regular Reports
- Weekly: Communication frequency and employee feedback highlights
- Bi-weekly: Role clarity pulse survey results with trend analysis
- Monthly: Comprehensive productivity and engagement metrics
- Quarterly: Full change management scorecard with recommendations

### Milestone Reviews
- Week 2: Initial implementation assessment
- Month 1: First month effectiveness review
- Month 3: Quarter 1 comprehensive evaluation
- Month 6: Mid-transition impact analysis
- Month 12: Full-year post-change assessment
