### üí¨ RECOMMENDATION #1: Launch Weekly Communication Rhythm

**The Big Idea:** Create a consistent cadence of executive communication that employees can count on.

#### **What You'll Actually Do:**

**1Ô∏è‚É£ Weekly All-Hands (15 minutes, Thursdays)**
- CEO or leadership team member
- Format: 10 min update + 5 min Q&A
- Content: This week's priorities, decision context, "here's what it means for you"

**2Ô∏è‚É£ Monthly Town Hall (45 minutes, 1st Wednesday)**
- Full executive team
- Format: 20 min updates + 25 min Q&A
- Content: Strategic updates, metrics, upcoming changes

**3Ô∏è‚É£ Manager Cascade (within 24 hours)**
- Every manager holds 15-min team meeting after exec communication
- Purpose: Translate exec message into "here's what this means for OUR team"

#### **Why This Works:**
- ‚úÖ Scientific: d=0.47 effect on role clarity
- ‚úÖ Organizational: r=.45-.55 correlation proven
- ‚úÖ Practitioner: European bank + Microsoft both did this successfully
- ‚úÖ Stakeholder: Employees specifically asked for this

#### **Rollout Timeline:**

| Phase | Duration | What Happens |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| **Prep** | Weeks 1-4 | Get buy-in, design templates, train execs & managers |
| **Pilot** | Months 2-3 | Test with one division, gather feedback, refine |
| **Scale** | Months 4-6 | Roll out to full organization |
| **Results** | Month 12 | Assess ROI and adjust |

#### **Expected Results:**
- üìä **3 months:** 10-15% role clarity improvement
- üìà **6 months:** 5-10% engagement boost, lower turnover
- üí∞ **12 months:** 10-20% productivity increase

#### **Common Obstacles (And How To Beat Them):**

| "But..." | Solution |
|----------|----------|
| "Execs are too busy!" | Pre-block calendar for full year; designate backups |
| "More meetings?!" | Replace old meetings, don't add new ones |
| "What if execs bomb the Q&A?" | Train them; start with pre-submitted questions |
| "Managers won't cascade consistently" | Track it; tie to performance reviews |
| "This is flavor of the month" | Acknowledge past failures; commit to 12-month minimum |

#### **Success Looks Like:**
- ‚úÖ 90%+ executive attendance consistency
- ‚úÖ Role clarity improves ‚â•10% within 6 months
- ‚úÖ 80%+ manager cascade compliance
