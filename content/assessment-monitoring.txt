## ÔøΩ EVALUATION DESIGN: Treatment vs. Comparison Groups

### Why This Approach?

**Ideal:** Randomized controlled trial (RCT)  
**Reality:** Can't randomly assign communication in one organization  
**Solution:** Quasi-experimental design with matched comparison group

This lets us rule out alternative explanations (economy, seasonality, etc.)

---

### The Setup

| Aspect | Treatment Group | Comparison Group |
|--------|----------------|------------------|
| **Who** | Division A (200-300 employees) | Division B (similar size/function) |
| **Gets Intervention?** | ‚úÖ YES (weekly/monthly comms) | ‚ùå NO (standard quarterly updates) |
| **Gets Measured?** | ‚úÖ YES (quarterly surveys) | ‚úÖ YES (same surveys - controls for measurement effects) |
| **Timeline** | Enhanced comms start Month 2 | Standard comms continue through Month 12 |

**Key:** Groups are **matched** on:
- Similar department functions
- Similar team sizes
- Similar baseline clarity scores
- Similar baseline productivity
- Similar manager experience

**After Month 12:** Comparison group gets the intervention (ethical + replication test)
